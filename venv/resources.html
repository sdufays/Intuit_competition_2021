Diverse Resources


Empathizing is one of the defining traits of humans. The ability to listen and attempt to understand and relate to someone’s emotions is just one of the biggest ways we show compassion to others, and it’s one of the biggest skills in use when we share our experiences with others. When we listen and share our stories with others, we are able to understand each other in new, deeper ways. Furthermore, it allows us to identify with others, creating a safe haven of communication in what can often be dark stories of racism, sexism, or any form of hatred and discrimination. It’s important to listen to those who share their stories, as it is their stories that give us the information needed to identify the root cause of the issue, discover the extent of its effects on others, and collaboratively create ways to solve the problem.
